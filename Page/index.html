Who We Are...

BooksLuva is an inimitable platform to Buy/Sell/Renting College books.

The journey started from the year 2017 by hand in hand delivery of books to engineering students after knowing that they face a bit trouble in buying books in remote area. It is a proud moment to say that BooksLuva is the first start-up of GEC, Patan to get a Start-up fund under SSIP Policy of Government of Gujarat.
